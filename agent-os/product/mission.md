# Pebble Sales Hub - Product Mission

## Problem Statement

A startup company sells products through social media campaigns (Facebook posts, Instagram stories, live videos). As the business grew, manual tracking with Excel and paper created:

- **Inaccurate sales records** - Orders lost or miscounted across campaigns
- **Commission disputes** - Errors in calculating sales person payouts
- **Time wasted** - Hours spent reconciling spreadsheets each month
- **No visibility** - Sales persons couldn't see their own performance

## Solution

Pebble Sales Hub provides a centralized system where:

- Admins manage sales team members with configurable commission rates
- Campaigns are created for each social media post, live, or event
- Orders are recorded against campaigns with automatic commission calculation
- Commission rates are "snapshotted" at order creation for accuracy
- Sales persons view their earnings broken down by campaign and month

## Target Users

### Admin
- **Role**: Business owner or manager
- **Goals**: Manage team, track all sales, ensure accurate payouts
- **Pain points**: Spending hours on Excel, commission calculation errors, no real-time overview
- **Key actions**: Create users, set commission rates, manage campaigns, view team payouts

### Sales Person
- **Role**: Sales team member handling social media campaigns
- **Goals**: See assigned campaigns, record orders quickly, track personal earnings
- **Pain points**: Not knowing commission earned, unclear which campaigns performed well
- **Key actions**: View campaigns, create orders, check monthly payout breakdown
